01 The Digital Crisis

In the age of hyper-connectivity, women are increasingly targeted by predatory psychological tactics including Love Bombing, Sextortion, and Financial Coercion. Traditional security tools fail because they scan for malicious links, not malicious behavior.

02 How ConvoShield Protects

Real-Time Watchdog

Continuous WhatsApp Web monitoring via our specialized Chrome Extension that detects and alerts on suspicious chat spikes.

Vision Analytics

Proprietary OCR system (Tesseract.js) allowing users to analyze screenshots directly, bypassing manual data entry.

Cognitive Engine

Deep-learning analysis of conversation tropes using NVIDIA's nim for low-latency, high-accuracy threat detection.

Emergency Loop

Immediate linkage to National Cyber Cell 1930 and gender-specific support networks integrated into the analysis UI.

05 Security Library & Encyclopedia

Empowerment through Awareness

ConvoShield doesn't just scan; it teaches. Our integrated Security Library provides a real-time feed of trending Indian scams (Digital Arrest, AI Voice Clones) ensuring users are one step ahead of predators.
