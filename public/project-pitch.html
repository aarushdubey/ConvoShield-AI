The Problem

"Every 10 minutes, a woman in India faces digital harassment or cyber-extortion. Most victims feel isolated and don't know how to differentiate a genuine conversation from a manipulative scam."

Digital platforms have become a breeding ground for Love Bombing, Sextortion, and Advance-Fee scams. Traditional security tools block links, but they can't understand the psychological tactics used in chat conversations.

The Solution

ConvoShield AI acts as a cognitive guardian. It uses advanced Large Language Models to analyze chat history and detect patterns of manipulation before harm occurs.
